Fruit Smoothie

Are you ready to turn your health up a notch? You've come to the right place! This fruit smoothie will have you feeling rejuvenated in no time! The best part? it tastes delicious! Get these items ready ASAP:

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp orange juice
  • 1 tsp milk

Now don't mess this up, it's very important and even more difficult!

Grab all of your ingredients, throw them into your blender, and blend until smooth...oh, that's it! Viola, you're done and now have a deliciously healthy smoothie to sip down.
