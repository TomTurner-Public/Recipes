Seared Salmon

So you want to learn how to pan-sear some salmon to make a delicious seafood-centric plate? Look no further!

Get these ingredients ready before you begin cookin'!
  • Four 6 oz filet salmon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 slices lemon
