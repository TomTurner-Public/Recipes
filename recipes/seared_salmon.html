Seared Salmon

So you want to learn how to pan-sear some salmon to make a delicious seafood-centric plate? Look no further!

Get these ingredients ready before you begin cookin'!
  • Four 6 oz filet salmon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 slices lemon

Great, now we have our salmon and it's delicious toppings ready. It's time to turn that pan on and get to it.

  1. Preheat your skillet over medium heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Coat your salmon in olive oil and place skin side down on pan; increase heat to high.
  3. Sprinkle salmon with salt, pepper, and capers. Cook for 3 minutes on skin side before flipping over.
  4. Continue cooking other side of salmon until it easily flakes with fork (roughly 5 or so minutes).
  5. Turn heat off, lay your salmon and your plate, and garnish with lemon slices. Enjoy!
